About Temple

A Hindu temple, or mandir or koil in Indian languages, is a house, seat, and body of divinity for Hindus. Peacock

The peacock, known for its vibrant plumage and majestic appearance, is considered a sacred bird in Hinduism. It is associated with various deities and is often depicted in temple art and sculpture. The peacock symbolizes beauty, grace, and pride, and its feathers are used in religious ceremonies and rituals.
